Bio: What is Austin Harmony Chorus?

Austin Harmony Chorus is an award winning a cappella barbershop chorus of women ranging in age from 20's to 80's and all with diverse backgrounds, careers and interests. Austin Harmony Chorus's remarkable journey began with a vision shared by six women, and chartered as a chapter of Sweet Adelines International with 27 members on October 17, 2005. Sweet Adelines International is a highly respected worldwide organization of women singers committed to advancing the musical art form of barbershop harmony through education, competition and performance. Sweet Adelines choruses and quartets sing exciting a cappella, four-part harmony music, barbershop style.
